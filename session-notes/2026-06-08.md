### Remote sensing methods rationale
- Sentinel-1 SAR VV: surface dielectric → soil moisture proxy
- VH/VV ratio: reduces vegetation volume scattering, isolates soil signal
- GRACE-FO TWS: full water column (soil + groundwater + snow) via gravity anomaly
- PALSAR-2 (JAXA L-band): noted as future enhancement — better vegetation penetration, requires separate data acquisition
